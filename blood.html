Blood Donation

Blood is the most precious gift that we can give to others and it's essential for our survival in this world of suffering.
The survey reports has, India's annual blood requirement is 26.2 million units. In every two secounds, a patient in India needs blood and out of every three people will need blood in their lifetime. A person has a five to six litres of blood in his or her body and can donate blood every 90 days.
